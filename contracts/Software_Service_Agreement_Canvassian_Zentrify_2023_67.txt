**RECITALS**

WHEREAS, Provider is in the business of developing and providing software solutions and services; and

WHEREAS, Client desires to obtain certain software services from Provider as detailed below.

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements herein contained, the parties agree as follows:

**1. Services Provided**

Provider agrees to provide the following software services to Client:

1.1 Development of custom software applications tailored to Client’s business needs.

1.2 Integration of existing solutions with new software applications.

1.3 Ongoing maintenance and support of the provided software.

1.4 Training sessions for Client’s staff on effective utilization of the software.

**2. Term**

This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and shall continue for a period of one (1) year. The Agreement may be extended upon mutual written consent of both parties.

**3. Compensation**

3.1 Fees. Client agrees to pay Provider the sum of AUD $250,000 for the services described in Section 1.

3.2 Payment Schedule. The payment shall be made in four equal installments of AUD $62,500, due on the 15th day of each quarter following the Effective Date.

3.3 Expenses. Client agrees to reimburse Provider for all reasonable out-of-pocket expenses incurred in connection with services provided, with prior approval from Client.

**4. Confidentiality**

4.1 Each party agrees to keep confidential all proprietary or confidential information of the other party and to use such information solely for the purposes of this Agreement.

4.2 Neither party shall disclose such confidential information to any third party without the prior written consent of the other party.

**5. Intellectual Property Rights**

5.1 All intellectual property rights in the software, materials, and any modifications thereof are and shall remain the property of Provider.

5.2 Client is granted a non-exclusive, non-transferable, and limited right to use the software solely for its internal business purposes.

**6. Warranties and Representations**

6.1 Provider warrants that the software will perform substantially in accordance with the documentation provided to Client.

6.2 Provider does not warrant that the software will be error-free or uninterrupted.

**7. Limitation of Liability**

7.1 In no event shall Provider be liable to Client for any indirect, incidental, consequential, or punitive damages arising out of or related to this Agreement.

7.2 Provider's total liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the total fees paid by Client to Provider.

**8. Termination**

8.1 Either party may terminate this Agreement upon thirty (30) days’ prior written notice if the other party is in material breach of any term of this Agreement and such breach remains uncured at the end of the notice period.

8.2 Upon termination, Client shall pay Provider for all services rendered up to the effective date of termination.

**9. Governing Law**

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of New South Wales, Australia, without regard to its conflict of laws principles.

10.2 Amendments. This Agreement may only be amended by a written document duly executed by both parties.
