**CONTRACT FOR SOFTWARE SERVICES**

This Software Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of this 1st day of October, 2023 (the "Effective Date"), by and between Canvassian Pty Ltd., an Australian Software Company with its principal place of business at 123 Innovation Drive, Sydney, Australia ("Supplier"), and Globex Corporation, with its principal place of business at 456 Enterprise Road, Melbourne, Australia ("Client").

**1. Services Provided**

1.1 Scope of Services. Supplier agrees to provide the software development, maintenance, and related services detailed in Exhibit A ("Services").

1.2 Deliverables. Supplier shall deliver to Client the software applications, updates, and documents described in Exhibit B ("Deliverables") according to the timeline outlined in Exhibit C ("Delivery Schedule").

**2. Payment Terms**

2.1 Fees. Client shall pay Supplier the fees set forth in Exhibit D ("Fees"). Fees shall be payable in the amounts and according to the schedule specified in the same exhibit.

2.2 Invoices. Supplier will invoice Client monthly for the Services performed and Deliverables provided during the preceding month. Client agrees to pay each invoice within thirty (30) days of receipt.

2.3 Late Payment. Any late payment shall incur a fee of 1.5% per month, or the highest amount allowed by law, whichever is lower. In addition, Supplier may suspend work until payment is received.

**3. Term and Termination**

3.1 Term. This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue for a period of one (1) year, unless earlier terminated as provided herein.

3.2 Renewal. Upon mutual agreement, the parties may renew this Agreement for additional one-year terms.

3.3 Termination for Cause. Either party may terminate this Agreement for cause if the other party fails to cure a material breach within thirty (30) days of receipt of written notice.

3.4 Termination for Convenience. Either party may terminate this Agreement for convenience upon sixty (60) days' written notice.

**4. Confidentiality**

4.1 Confidential Information. Each party agrees to hold the other party’s Confidential Information in strict confidence and not to disclose such Confidential Information to any third party, except as required by law.

4.2 Use of Confidential Information. Confidential Information shall be used solely for the purposes of fulfilling the obligations under this Agreement.

**5. Intellectual Property**

5.1 Ownership. Supplier retains ownership of all pre-existing intellectual property it brings to the project. Client shall own new intellectual property developed specifically under this Agreement upon full payment of Fees.

5.2 License. Supplier grants Client a non-exclusive, non-transferable license to use the pre-existing intellectual property included within the Deliverables.

7.1 Limitation of Liability. IN NO EVENT SHALL SUPPLIER BE LIABLE FOR ANY INDIRECT, INCIDENTAL, SPECIAL, CONSEQUENTIAL, OR PUNITIVE DAMAGES ARISING OUT OF OR RELATED TO THIS AGREEMENT.

7.2 Cap on Liability. Supplier’s total liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the total amount of Fees paid by Client.
