2.1 This Agreement shall commence on the date first written above and continue for an initial term of one (1) year, unless terminated earlier in accordance with the terms of this Agreement.

3.1 The Client shall pay the Service Provider for the Services at the rates set forth in Schedule B.
3.2 Invoices will be issued monthly and are payable within thirty (30) days of the invoice date.

4.1 Each party agrees to maintain the confidentiality of any proprietary or confidential information disclosed by the other party under this Agreement.
4.2 The obligations of confidentiality shall survive termination of this Agreement.

6.2 The Client shall indemnify and hold harmless the Service Provider from and against any and all claims, liabilities, losses, damages, and expenses arising out of or in connection with the Client’s use of the Services.

7.1 To the maximum extent permitted by law, the Service Provider’s total liability to the Client for any cause of action arising out of or in connection with this Agreement shall not exceed the total fees paid by the Client to the Service Provider under this Agreement.
