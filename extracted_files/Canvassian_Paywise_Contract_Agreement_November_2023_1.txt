**CONTRACT AGREEMENT**

**THIS CONTRACT is made on this 15th day of November 2023**

**BETWEEN**

**Canvassian Pty Ltd**, a company registered in Australia with its registered office at 12 Innovation Way, Sydney, NSW 2000 (hereinafter referred to as "Supplier")

AND

**Paywise**, a company registered in Australia with its registered office at 45 Financial Rd, Sydney, NSW 2000 (hereinafter referred to as "Client")

**WHEREAS**, the Supplier is engaged in the business of developing and providing software solutions and has expertise in (specifically) financial management software;

**AND WHEREAS**, the Client desires to purchase certain software solutions from the Supplier and the Supplier agrees to provide these services under the terms set forth in this agreement.

**NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants contained herein, the parties agree as follows:**

**1. SCOPE OF WORK**
1.1. The Supplier shall provide the Client with the software solution known as "Paywise Financial Suite" (hereafter referred to as the "Software").
1.2. The Supplier agrees to deliver the Software and support services which include installation, configuration, and user training (the "Services") as detailed in Exhibit A attached hereto.

**2. TERM**
2.1. This Contract shall commence on the effective date of this Agreement and continue for a period of three (3) years, unless terminated earlier in accordance with the provisions contained herein.

**3. FEES AND PAYMENT**
3.1. The total fee for the Software and Services shall be AUD 500,000.
3.2. Payment shall occur in three installments:
   - 1st installment of AUD 200,000 upon signing this Contract.
   - 2nd installment of AUD 150,000 upon delivery of the Software.
   - 3rd installment of AUD 150,000 upon completion of training.

**4. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY**
4.1. The Supplier retains all rights, title, and interest in the Software.
4.2. The Client shall not sublicense, distribute or share the Software without prior written consent from the Supplier.

**5. CONFIDENTIALITY**
5.1. Both parties agree to keep confidential any information that is disclosed as a part of this agreement that is not publicly available.

**6. TERMINATION**
6.1. Either party may terminate the Contract if the other party materially breaches its terms and fails to cure the breach within thirty (30) days following written notice.

**7. GOVERNING LAW**
7.1. This Contract shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of New South Wales.

**IN WITNESS WHEREOF**, the parties hereto have caused this Contract to be executed by themselves or their duly authorized representatives as of the date first above written.
