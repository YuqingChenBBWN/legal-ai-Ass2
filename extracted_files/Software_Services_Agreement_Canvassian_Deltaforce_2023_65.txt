**Software Services Agreement**

**This Software Services Agreement ("Agreement") is made and entered into this 1st day of October, 2023 (the "Effective Date") by and between:**

**Canvassian Pty Ltd**, an Australian corporation having its principal place of business at 45 Innovation Drive, Sydney, NSW 2000, Australia ("Canvassian" or "Provider")

and

**Deltaforce Corporation**, a corporation having its principal place of business at 123 Market Street, Melbourne, VIC 3000, Australia ("Deltaforce" or "Client").

**WHEREAS:**

a) Canvassian is engaged in the business of providing software solutions and related services; and 

b) Deltaforce desires to engage Canvassian to provide certain software services under the terms and conditions set forth herein;

**NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual promises contained herein and other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged, the parties hereby agree as follows:**

**1. Definitions**

1.1 "Services" means the software services and deliverables specified in Exhibit A attached hereto.

1.2 "Deliverables" means the outcomes and products to be delivered by Canvassian as specified in Exhibit A.

**2. Scope of Services**

2.1 Canvassian agrees to perform the Services for Deltaforce as described in Exhibit A with the highest professional standards.

2.2 Any changes or additions to the Services or Deliverables must be set forth in writing and signed by both parties.

**3. Term and Termination**

3.1 This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and shall continue for a period of twelve (12) months unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Agreement.

3.2 Either party may terminate this Agreement with or without cause by giving thirty (30) days' written notice to the other party.

3.3 Upon termination of this Agreement for any reason, Canvassian shall cease all work on behalf of Deltaforce and shall deliver to Deltaforce all completed deliverables and work in progress.

**4. Fees and Payment**

4.1 In consideration for the Services, Deltaforce shall pay Canvassian the fees as set forth in Exhibit B attached hereto.

4.2 Invoices shall be submitted by Canvassian on a monthly basis and shall be payable within thirty (30) days of receipt.

4.3 All fees are exclusive of GST, VAT, or other local taxes which shall be borne by Deltaforce.

**5. Confidentiality**

5.1 Each party agrees to keep the other party's Confidential Information secure and not to disclose it to any third party except as required by law.

5.2 "Confidential Information" shall include all information disclosed by either party identified as confidential at the time of disclosure or that reasonably should be understood to be confidential.

**6. Intellectual Property Rights**

6.1 Canvassian retains all intellectual property rights in the software and methodologies used in providing the Services.

6.2 Deltaforce shall have a limited, non-exclusive license to use the software developed by Canvassian solely for its internal business purposes.

**7. Warranties and Disclaimers**

7.1 Canvassian warrants that the Services will be performed in a professional and workmanlike manner.

7.2 Except as expressly stated herein, Canvassian makes no other warranties, express or implied, including but not limited to, the implied warranties of merchantability and fitness for a particular purpose.

**8. Limitation of Liability**

8.1 In no event shall either party be liable to the other for any indirect, special, incidental, or consequential damages, arising out of or related to this Agreement.

8.2 Canvassian’s total liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the amount of fees paid by Deltaforce to Canvassian in the twelve (12) months preceding the event giving rise to the claim.

**9. Miscellaneous**

9.1 This Agreement sets forth the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior discussions, agreements, or understandings of any kind.

9.2 This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of New South Wales, Australia.

9.3 Any notices required under this Agreement shall be in writing and sent to the addresses specified above.

9.4 This Agreement may be executed in counterparts, each of which shall be deemed an original, and together they shall constitute one and the same agreement.
