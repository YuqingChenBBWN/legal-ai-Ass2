**Software Development and Services Agreement**

**This Agreement** is made and entered into as of the 1st day of September, 2023 by and between:

**Canvassian Pty Ltd**, an Australian company with its principal place of business at Level 12, 34 Software Street, Sydney, NSW, Australia (hereinafter referred to as "Service Provider")

**and**

**Bravocat Pty Ltd**, an Australian company with its principal place of business at Level 3, 21 Innovation Way, Melbourne, VIC, Australia (hereinafter referred to as "Client").

**WHEREAS**, Service Provider is engaged in the business of providing software development and related services;

**WHEREAS**, Client desires to retain Service Provider to provide such services on the terms and conditions set forth herein,

**NOW, THEREFORE**, in consideration of the mutual promises herein contained, the parties hereto agree as follows:

### 1. **Scope of Services**

1.1 **Description of Services**: Service Provider shall provide software development and related services as described in Exhibit A attached hereto (the "Services").

1.2 **Performance Standards**: Service Provider shall perform the Services in a diligent, professional, and timely manner, consistent with industry standards and practices.

### 2. **Term and Termination**

2.1 **Term**: This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and shall continue for a period of three (3) years unless terminated earlier in accordance with the provisions of this Agreement.

2.2 **Termination for Convenience**: Either party may terminate this Agreement for convenience with thirty (30) days’ written notice to the other party.

2.3 **Termination for Cause**: Either party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if the other party materially breaches any provision of this Agreement and fails to cure such breach within fifteen (15) days after receiving written notice thereof.

### 3. **Fees and Payment**

3.1 **Service Fees**: In consideration for the Services to be performed by Service Provider, Client shall pay to Service Provider the fees as set forth in Exhibit B.

3.2 **Payment Terms**: Payment shall be made within thirty (30) days from the date of receipt of a proper invoice from Service Provider.

3.3 **Late Payments**: Any payment not received within such period shall accrue interest at a rate of 1.5% per month (or the highest rate permitted by law, if lower).

### 4. **Confidentiality**

4.1 **Confidential Information**: Both parties agree to hold in confidence all confidential information disclosed by the other party.

4.2 **Obligations**: Confidential information shall not be disclosed to any third party without the prior written consent of the disclosing party, except as required by law.

### 5. **Change in Control**

5.1 **Notification of Change in Control**: Each party agrees to notify the other party in writing at least thirty (30) days prior to any proposed change in control. 

5.2 **Right to Terminate**: In the event of a change in control of either party, the other party shall have the right to terminate this Agreement with thirty (30) days’ written notice.

5.3 **Definition**: For the purpose of this section, "change in control" shall mean any event in which 50% or more of the voting shares or ownership interests of a party are transferred to a new owner.

### 6. **Intellectual Property Rights**

6.1 **Ownership**: All intellectual property rights in any software, documentation, or other materials developed under this Agreement shall be owned by Client, except as otherwise agreed in writing.

6.2 **License**: Service Provider hereby grants Client a perpetual, irrevocable, non-exclusive license to use all intellectual property developed under this Agreement.

### 7. **Indemnification**

7.1 **Indemnity**: Each party shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless the other party from any claims, damages, liabilities, and expenses arising out of or related to any breach of this Agreement.

### 8. **Miscellaneous**

8.1 **Governing Law**: This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of New South Wales, Australia.

8.2 **Entire Agreement**: This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior or contemporaneous agreements and understandings.

8.3 **Amendments**: Any amendment or modification of this Agreement must be in writing and signed by both parties.

8.4 **Notices**: All notices required or permitted under this Agreement shall be in writing and shall be deemed delivered when delivered in person or deposited in the Australian mail, postage prepaid, addressed to the respective party at the address set forth above.
