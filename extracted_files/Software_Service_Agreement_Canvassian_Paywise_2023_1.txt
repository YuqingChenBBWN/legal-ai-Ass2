**Software Service Agreement**

This Software Service Agreement ("Agreement") is made and entered into as of October 1, 2023 ("Effective Date"), by and between Canvassian Pty Ltd., an Australian company with its principal place of business at 123 Innovation Drive, Sydney, NSW 2000, Australia ("Canvassian"), and Paywise Limited, an Australian company with its principal place of business at 456 Finance Avenue, Melbourne, VIC 3000, Australia ("Paywise").

**Recitals**

WHEREAS, Canvassian is in the business of developing and providing software solutions;

WHEREAS, Paywise wishes to engage Canvassian for certain software services as described herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual promises herein contained, the parties hereby agree as follows:

### 1. Definitions

1.1 "Services" means the software development, maintenance, and support services to be provided by Canvassian to Paywise as described in Exhibit A.

1.2 "Deliverables" means any reports, software, or other work products delivered by Canvassian to Paywise under this Agreement.

1.3 "Confidential Information" means any information disclosed by either party to the other party that is designated as confidential or that reasonably should be understood to be confidential.

### 2. Scope of Services

2.1 Canvassian agrees to provide Paywise with the Services described in Exhibit A attached hereto and made a part hereof.

2.2 Canvassian shall use commercially reasonable efforts to meet the timelines and deliverables specified in Exhibit A.

### 3. Compensation

3.1 Paywise agrees to pay Canvassian a total fee of AUD $500,000 for the Services provided under this Agreement.

3.2 Payment is to be made in the following schedule:
   - 25% upon signing of this Agreement.
   - 25% upon delivery of the first milestone as detailed in Exhibit A.
   - 25% upon delivery of the second milestone.
   - 25% upon final acceptance of all deliverables.

3.3 Payments shall be due thirty (30) days from the date of invoice. Late payments will incur a late fee of 1.5% per month.

### 4. Term and Termination

4.1 This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue until all Services are satisfactorily performed, unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Section.

4.2 Either party may terminate this Agreement for any reason upon thirty (30) days written notice to the other party.

4.3 Either party may terminate this Agreement immediately if the other party breaches any material term of this Agreement and fails to cure the breach within fifteen (15) days.

### 5. Confidentiality

5.1 Each party agrees to protect the other party's Confidential Information using the same degree of care that it uses to protect its own Confidential Information but in no event less than reasonable care.

5.2 Confidential Information shall not include information that:
   - is or becomes publicly known through no act of the receiving party;
   - was in the receiving party's possession before receipt from the disclosing party;
   - is rightfully received by the receiving party from a third party without breach of any duty of confidentiality;
   - is independently developed by the receiving party.

### 6. Intellectual Property

6.1 All intellectual property rights developed by Canvassian under this Agreement shall be the sole property of Canvassian. Paywise shall have a non-exclusive, non-transferable license to use the Deliverables solely for its internal business purposes.

### 7. Warranties and Disclaimers

7.1 Canvassian warrants that the Services will be performed in a professional and workmanlike manner.

7.2 Canvassian does not warrant that the Deliverables will be error-free or that they will meet Paywise’s particular requirements.

### 8. Limitation of Liability

8.1 In no event shall either party be liable for any indirect, incidental, special, or consequential damages arising out of this Agreement.

8.2 Canvassian’s liability for any claims arising out of this Agreement shall not exceed the total amount paid by Paywise to Canvassian under this Agreement.

### 9. General Provisions

9.1 Governing Law. This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of New South Wales, Australia.

9.2 Entire Agreement. This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior negotiations, representations, and agreements between them.

9.3 Amendments. No amendment or modification of this Agreement shall be valid unless in writing and signed by both parties.

9.4 Severability. If any provision of this Agreement is held to be invalid or unenforceable, such provision shall be struck and the remaining provisions shall continue in full force and effect.

9.5 Waiver. No waiver of any breach of any provision of this Agreement shall constitute a waiver of any prior, concurrent, or subsequent breach of the same or any other provisions, and no waiver shall be effective unless made in writing and signed by an authorized representative of the waiving party.
